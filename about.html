BIO

Eros Gaspardo (b. 1997) is a photographer based in Italy. After taking a BA in Preservation of Cultural Heritage at Ca’ Foscari University of Venice he is currently undertaking the MA in Photography at ISIA U. Mixing digital and analog photography the main focus of his practice lies in landscape, conceptual and fashion photography.
