Life, Still
2023

The project Life, Still was born within the Archivio Atena artistic residency realized in the town of Atena Lucana (SA). Life, Still is configured as a catalogue of the rooms and objects present inside a single house located in the historic town center, Casa Italia, as it is affectionately called by the inhabitants. However, it is not just a regular house: although it has been uninhabited for more than two years now, the house seems to retain a vitality of its own, both thanks to the numerous objects, preserved unaltered, that are still inside it and to the immaculate state of preservation of the rooms. I meticulously catalogued the individual objects that make up the rooms in a neutral way using a single frontal point of view so as to suspend them in time and space, making them identifiable by all as objects belonging to a common memory. The rooms, on the other hand, were documented differently through an almost oblique shot returning the feeling of a house still inhabited and pulsating with life. Souvenir photos, furnishings, kitchen utensils, but also keys, documents, medicines and small objects that become a kind of personal, collective and social biography that recaptures habits, desires and memories
