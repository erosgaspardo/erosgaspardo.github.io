Incomplete Itinerary: 24 Contemporary Churches in Milan
2023

Since 1945 and throughout the great economic boom, the Ambrosian diocese's land and its surroundings were enriched with churches. Milan alone saw the rise of more than 300 churches. At that time, spaces represented the expression of experimentation of well-known architects; today, through the work of 12 Photography students within the Architecture Photography course held by Alberto Sinigaglia, an attempt is made to investigate through images the well-known works of architects between the cities of Milan, Varese and Pavia. "Incomplete itinerary: 24 contemporary churches in Lombardy" draws an itinerary intended to lead to a route that is not purely ordinary. In the exhibition at Rumore and in the publication, one is guided by a geographical map that locates the churches in the three areas explored, describing the incomplete route to follow.
